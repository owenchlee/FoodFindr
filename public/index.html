          FAQ & disclaimers

          Are the dish suggestions real?

          Claude reads each restaurant's actual reviews and only suggests a dish the reviews genuinely mention. If nothing specific comes up, it says so honestly instead of inventing one.

          Is the price filter exact?

          It's a ceiling based on Google's 0–4 price tier, not an exact per-item price — the $/$$/$$$ ranges shown are approximate, per-person guides, not guarantees.

          Does "Sharing" show real prices?

          No. Google doesn't provide real menu prices, so we only calculate a total budget for the group to guide how many items make sense — we never invent or display a per-item dollar figure.

          How accurate are dietary restriction matches?

          Best-effort only, based on whatever reviews happen to mention — there's no real ingredient or allergen data, so always double-check with the restaurant directly.

          What does the progress bar count?

          Restaurants FoodFindr has shown you in your city so far — not every restaurant that exists. It grows as you search rather than starting from a true city-wide total.

          What counts toward my streak?

          Any day you log at least one visit, based on UTC calendar days — logging twice in one day only counts once.

          How is the leaderboard ranked?

          By total visits logged, all-time. Streaks are shown alongside but don't affect rank — a missed day shouldn't tank your spot.
